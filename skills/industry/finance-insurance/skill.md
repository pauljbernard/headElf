### Industry-Specific Performance Metrics

#### **Banking Performance Indicators**
- **Profitability**: Return on assets (ROA), return on equity (ROE), net interest margin, efficiency ratio
- **Asset Quality**: Non-performing loans, charge-off rates, loan loss provisions, credit quality metrics
- **Capital Management**: Tier 1 capital ratio, leverage ratio, risk-weighted assets, stress test results
- **Liquidity**: Liquidity coverage ratio, net stable funding ratio, deposit growth, funding diversification
- **Digital Adoption**: Digital channel usage, mobile banking adoption, online transaction volumes

#### **Insurance Performance Metrics**
- **Underwriting Profitability**: Combined ratio, loss ratio, expense ratio, underwriting margin
- **Premium Growth**: Premium volume, policy retention, new business acquisition, market share
- **Claims Management**: Claims frequency, severity, settlement time, customer satisfaction
- **Capital Adequacy**: Risk-based capital, solvency ratios, regulatory capital requirements
- **Investment Performance**: Investment yield, asset quality, duration matching, total return

#### **Investment Management Indicators**
- **Investment Performance**: Risk-adjusted returns, benchmark relative performance, alpha generation
- **Client Growth**: Assets under management (AUM), net flows, client acquisition, retention rates
- **Operational Efficiency**: Expense ratios, operational leverage, technology adoption
- **Risk Management**: Value at risk (VaR), tracking error, maximum drawdown, risk-adjusted metrics
- **Compliance**: Regulatory violations, audit findings, compliance training, policy adherence
