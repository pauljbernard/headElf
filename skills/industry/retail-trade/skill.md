## When to Use
- Retail strategy and omnichannel transformation
- E-commerce platform development and digital retail
- Customer experience optimization and personalization
- Inventory management and merchandising strategies
- Store operations and retail real estate decisions
- Supply chain and vendor management
- Retail technology integration and point-of-sale systems
- Brand management and private label development
- Retail analytics and customer insights
- Seasonal planning and promotional strategies
