##### **Quality Gates and Decision Blockers**

```
CEO Decision Quality Assurance → Executive Excellence Standards
├── Gate 1: Strategic Completeness (REQUIRED)
│   ├── ✅ Strategic Problem Definition: Clear, specific, measurable challenge identified
│   ├── ✅ Strategic Alternative Analysis: Minimum 3 options analyzed with equal rigor
│   ├── ✅ Stakeholder Impact Assessment: All major stakeholders considered with specific impacts
│   ├── ✅ Resource Requirement Specification: Specific budget, timeline, personnel with justification
│   └── ❌ BLOCK if any strategic component missing or inadequately analyzed
├── Gate 2: Financial Rigor (REQUIRED)
│   ├── ✅ Financial Impact Quantification: Specific dollar amounts with confidence ranges
│   ├── ✅ ROI Analysis: NPV, IRR, payback calculations with methodology shown
│   ├── ✅ Risk Quantification: P(failure) × $Impact = $Expected Loss for major risks
│   ├── ✅ Sensitivity Analysis: Key assumption impact on financial outcomes
│   └── ❌ BLOCK if financial analysis fabricated, incomplete, or lacks confidence calibration
├── Gate 3: Implementation Reality (REQUIRED)
│   ├── ✅ Execution Plan: Specific phases, milestones, deliverables with timeline
│   ├── ✅ Resource Availability: Confirmed budget, personnel, vendor capabilities
│   ├── ✅ Integration Complexity: Technical, organizational, market integration requirements
│   ├── ✅ Success Measurement: Specific KPIs, measurement methodology, monitoring systems
│   └── ❌ BLOCK if implementation plan abstract, resources unconfirmed, or success unmeasurable
├── Gate 4: Governance and Authority (REQUIRED)
│   ├── ✅ Authority Verification: Decision within CEO authority or board approval obtained
│   ├── ✅ Accountability Assignment: Named individuals accountable for each deliverable
│   ├── ✅ Board Communication: Board notification/approval appropriate to decision magnitude
│   ├── ✅ Escalation Framework: Clear triggers for escalation, modification, or termination
│   └── ❌ BLOCK if authority unclear, accountability missing, or governance inadequate
└── Decision Enhancement Requirements
    ├── Evidence Level Insufficient: Require additional data, analysis, or expert consultation
    ├── Stakeholder Input Missing: Require board input, investor consultation, employee feedback
    ├── Risk Assessment Incomplete: Require comprehensive risk analysis, mitigation planning
    ├── Implementation Plan Inadequate: Require detailed execution planning, resource confirmation
    └── Success Metrics Undefined: Require specific KPIs, measurement methodology, monitoring plan
```
