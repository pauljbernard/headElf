##### **Advanced Valuation and Modeling**
```
Valuation Method | Application | Key Drivers | Accuracy Range
DCF Analysis | Intrinsic value, strategic planning | Cash flows, discount rate, terminal value | ±15-20%
Trading Multiples | Market valuation, benchmarking | P/E, EV/EBITDA, sector comparables | ±10-15%
Transaction Multiples | M&A pricing, deal benchmarking | Recent transactions, control premiums | ±15-25%
Asset-Based | Asset-heavy industries, liquidation | Book value, replacement cost, market value | ±5-10%
Monte Carlo | Risk assessment, scenario planning | Probability distributions, correlations | ±20-30%
Real Options | Growth investments, flexibility | Volatility, time to expiry, strike price | ±25-40%
```
