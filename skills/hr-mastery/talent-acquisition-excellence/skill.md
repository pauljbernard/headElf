### Talent Acquisition Performance Metrics and Success Indicators

#### **Strategic Talent Acquisition KPIs**
- **Time-to-Fill**: Role posting to offer acceptance, segmented by role type and level
- **Cost-per-Hire**: Total recruiting cost divided by number of hires, including efficiency trends
- **Quality of Hire**: New hire performance, retention, and manager satisfaction scores
- **Offer Acceptance Rate**: Percentage of offers accepted, segmented by source and role
- **Candidate Satisfaction**: Application and interview experience ratings and feedback
- **Source Effectiveness**: Hire rate, quality, and retention by recruiting source

#### **Diversity and Inclusion KPIs**
- **Diverse Candidate Slate**: Percentage of diverse candidates in interview process
- **Diverse Hire Rate**: Percentage of diverse hires by demographic and level
- **Inclusive Process**: Bias incident rate, inclusive interviewing, and accessibility accommodation
- **Pipeline Diversity**: Diversity representation in candidate pipeline and talent pool
- **Advancement Rate**: Diverse talent promotion and advancement rates post-hire
- **Pay Equity**: Compensation analysis and gap identification for diverse talent

#### **Employer Brand and Experience KPIs**
- **Brand Awareness**: Employer brand recognition and perception measurement
- **Candidate Engagement**: Social media engagement, content sharing, and talent community growth
- **Application Rate**: Application volume and conversion from brand touchpoints
- **Employee Referral Rate**: Percentage of hires from employee referrals and advocacy
- **Glassdoor and Review Site Ratings**: Online employer reputation and review scores
- **Talent Community Growth**: Size and engagement level of talent pools and communities

#### **Operational Excellence KPIs**
- **Process Efficiency**: Automation utilization, workflow optimization, and error reduction
- **Recruiter Productivity**: Hire rate per recruiter, pipeline management, and activity metrics
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Hiring manager and candidate satisfaction scores
- **Technology Utilization**: ATS and tool adoption, user engagement, and ROI measurement
- **Compliance Rate**: Equal opportunity, privacy, and regulatory adherence metrics
- **Continuous Improvement**: Process improvement implementation and innovation adoption
