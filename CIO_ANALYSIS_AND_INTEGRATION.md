## Decision-Making Authorities and Responsibilities Specific to CIO

### Strategic Decision Matrix

#### **Level 1: Board/CEO Approval Required**
- **Strategic IT Investments** (>$5M annually)
  - ERP system implementations or replacements
  - Major data center relocations or cloud migrations
  - Enterprise-wide digital transformation initiatives
  - Significant vendor/service provider changes

- **Organizational Restructuring**
  - IT operating model changes
  - Major outsourcing decisions
  - Significant workforce reductions or expansions

- **Regulatory and Compliance Matters**
  - Major compliance violations or remediation
  - Significant security breaches or incidents
  - Regulatory audit findings and responses

#### **Level 2: CIO Authority with Executive Notification**
- **Operational IT Investments** ($1M - $5M)
  - Application purchases or upgrades
  - Infrastructure capacity expansions
  - Security tool implementations
  - Business intelligence platform investments

- **Vendor Management**
  - Contract negotiations (>$1M annual value)
  - Service provider performance management
  - Technology partnership agreements
  - Software license renewals and optimization

- **IT Strategy Execution**
  - Technology roadmap updates
  - Architecture standard changes
  - Service portfolio modifications
  - Digital workplace enhancements

#### **Level 3: CIO Authority with IT Governance Review**
- **Standard Operations** (<$1M annually)
  - Software tool procurements
  - Hardware refresh cycles
  - Service configuration changes
  - Process improvement initiatives

- **Team and Resource Management**
  - IT staffing decisions
  - Skills development programs
  - Performance management actions
  - Budget reallocation within approved limits

- **Policy and Standards**
  - IT policy updates
  - Security standard revisions
  - Service level agreement modifications
  - Compliance procedure updates

### Escalation Framework

#### **Immediate Escalation (24 hours)**

#### **Weekly Escalation**

## Governance and Compliance Requirements Specific to Information Management

### Information Governance Framework

#### **Data Governance Structure**
```
Data Governance Board (Executive Level)
├── CIO (Chair) - Strategic oversight and resource allocation
├── CDO/Chief Data Officer - Data strategy and policy development
├── CISO - Data security and privacy protection
├── Chief Legal Officer - Regulatory compliance and risk management
├── Business Unit Leaders - Domain expertise and requirements
└── Chief Analytics Officer - Analytics strategy and insights

Data Governance Committee (Operational Level)
├── Data Architects - Data model design and integration
├── Data Engineers - Data pipeline and infrastructure management
├── Data Scientists - Analytics and modeling requirements
├── Data Stewards - Data quality and domain expertise
├── Compliance Officers - Regulatory and policy compliance
└── IT Operations - Infrastructure and service management
```

#### **Information Classification and Handling**
- **Public Information**: Marketing materials, public reports, general company information
- **Internal Information**: Internal processes, procedures, employee communications
- **Confidential Information**: Customer data, financial information, strategic plans
- **Restricted Information**: Personal data, trade secrets, regulated data
- **Top Secret Information**: M&A information, executive compensation, security plans

#### **Data Privacy and Protection Requirements**
- **GDPR Compliance** (European Union):
  - Data protection by design and by default
  - Consent management and right to be forgotten
  - Data portability and access rights
  - Privacy impact assessments
  - Data protection officer designation

- **CCPA Compliance** (California):
  - Consumer privacy rights and disclosures
  - Opt-out mechanisms for data sale
  - Data inventory and mapping requirements
  - Privacy policy and notice requirements
  - Consumer request processing procedures

- **Industry-Specific Requirements**:
  - **HIPAA** (Healthcare): Protected health information safeguards
  - **PCI DSS** (Payment Cards): Cardholder data protection standards
  - **FERPA** (Education): Student record privacy protection
  - **GLBA** (Financial): Customer financial information protection
